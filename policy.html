Terms & Policies

Please read our policies carefully

Order Cancellation Policy

Photography Services:

  • Cancellations made 30+ days before event: Full refund minus 10% processing fee
  • Cancellations made 15-29 days before: 50% refund
  • Cancellations made 7-14 days before: 25% refund
  • Cancellations made less than 7 days before: No refund
  • Force majeure exceptions apply

Custom Products:

  • Cancellation before production starts: Full refund minus 10%
  • Cancellation after production starts: No refund
  • Production typically starts within 24 hours of order

How to Cancel:

Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your booking reference. Cancellation confirmation will be sent within 24 hours.

Advance Payment Policy

Photography Services:

  • 50% advance required to confirm booking
  • Balance due 7 days before event date
  • Payment methods: Bank transfer, mobile banking, cash, digital wallets

Custom Products:

  • 100% payment required before production
  • Bulk orders (10+ items): 50% advance, 50% before delivery

Payment Confirmation:

All payments are confirmed via WhatsApp. An invoice will be sent after payment verification. Please keep your payment receipt for reference.

Booking & Rescheduling Policy

Booking Confirmation:

  • Booking confirmed only after advance payment received
  • Confirmation message sent via WhatsApp
  • Event date reserved for confirmed bookings only

Rescheduling:

  • First reschedule: Free (if done 15+ days before event)
  • Subsequent reschedules: LKR 5,000 fee
  • Reschedules less than 15 days before: LKR 10,000 fee
  • Maximum 2 reschedules allowed
  • Subject to photographer availability

Weather/Emergency Rescheduling:

Outdoor shoots affected by severe weather: Free reschedule. Client emergency (medical, family): Considered on case-by-case basis.

Refund Policy

Refund Processing:

  • Approved refunds processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refund to original payment method
  • Processing fees are non-refundable

Non-Refundable Situations:

  • No-show on event day without prior notice
  • Client-caused delays exceeding agreed time
  • Refusal to cooperate during shoot
  • Force majeure on client side

Partial Refunds:

Unfulfilled service components will be refunded proportionally. Technical issues preventing complete delivery will be compensated accordingly.

Delivery & Usage Rights Policy

Photography Delivery:

  • Edited photos delivered within specified timeline in package
  • Delivery via online gallery, USB drive, or physical prints as ordered

Image Usage Rights:

  • Personal use: Unlimited
  • Commercial use: Requires separate license
  • PICTORA retains copyright for portfolio/marketing use
  • Client receives usage rights, not ownership
  • Images cannot be sold or distributed commercially without permission

Editing Revisions:

Minor edits included in package. Major re-edits subject to additional charges.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection:

We collect name, contact details, and event information solely for service delivery and communication. Your information is not shared with third parties without consent.

Photo Usage:

  • Some photos may be used in PICTORA portfolio/marketing
  • Clients can request photos not be shared publicly
  • Face blurring available on request for public sharing

Data Security:

  • Secure storage of client information
  • Photos stored for 1 year after delivery
  • Backup maintained for 6 months
  • Permanent deletion available on request

Have Questions About Our Policies?

Contact us for clarification on any of our terms and policies
